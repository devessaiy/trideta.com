Description of Service

TriDeta is an intelligent school management system providing tools for:

  • Student enrollment and biodata management
  • Academic result computation and report generation

The service relies on Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) to separate Admin, Teacher, Bursar, and Parent workflows, ensuring each role only accesses data appropriate to their function.
