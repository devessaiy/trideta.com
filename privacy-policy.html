Introduction

Welcome to TriDeta, a next-generation school management platform. TriDeta ("we," "our," or "us") respects the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application and platform.

This policy applies to School Administrators, Teachers, Bursars, Students, and Parents/Guardians using our services.

2

Information We Collect

We collect information that schools actively provide to us, as well as data generated through the use of our app.

  • User Account Information: To create accounts and enforce Role-Based Access Control (RBAC), we collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, and encrypted passwords. For some parent accounts, phone numbers may be used to generate internal unique login identifiers.
  • Student Data: School administrators and authorized staff input student information, which may include student biodata (names, date of birth, gender, addresses), admission numbers, profile photos, and guardian contact details.
  • Academic & Financial Records: We store academic performance data (exam scores, grades, affective domain records, report cards), attendance records, and financial data (fee structures, payment receipts, outstanding balances).
  • Device & Usage Data: For the purpose of sending school alerts and push notifications, we collect device identifiers (FCM tokens). We may also collect standard app crash logs and usage metrics to improve the platform.
🔐 Biometric Data: TriDeta offers fingerprint and Face ID login features. We do not store your biometric data on our servers. Biometric authentication is handled entirely locally on your device's secure hardware.
3

How We Use Your Information

We act as a Data Processor on behalf of the registered schools (who act as the Data Controllers). We use the collected data strictly to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the TriDeta platform.
  • Enable Role-Based Access Control — ensuring parents only see their children's data, while admins and bursars have appropriate school-wide access.
  • Generate academic dossiers, master broadsheets, and financial receipts.
  • Send push notifications and critical school alerts to your device.
  • Authenticate users securely via passwords, PINs, or biometric methods.
4

Data Sharing and Disclosure

🚫 We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Data is only shared in the following circumstances:

  • Within the School: Information is shared internally among authorized school staff (Admins, Teachers, Bursars) and Parents according to the school's configured permissions.
  • Service Providers: We share necessary data with trusted third-party infrastructure providers to host our databases securely (e.g., Supabase) and manage push notifications (e.g., Google Firebase).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
5

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including secure backend infrastructure (Supabase), encrypted passwords, and secure HTTPS connections, to protect your data.

However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6

Your Data Rights

Because TriDeta processes data on behalf of schools, parents and students seeking to access, correct, or delete their personal data should directly contact their School Administrator.

Administrators have the tools built into TriDeta to edit biodata, remove records, and manage parent credentials.

7

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy or via an in-app announcement.
